-====Class Array Indices====-
0 = Available Slots (✔)
	* CAN BE EMPTY FOR PREVIOUS-CLASS LABS
	* Can be put on hold (H)
	* Can be full (F)

1 = Enroll Count (✔)
	* CAN BE EMPTY

2 = Course Abbreviation/Name (✔)

3 = Course Number/ID (✔)

4 = Course Type (✔)
	* Typically either LAB or SEM, but LAB does not indicate that it is a previous-class lab or an individual-class lab.
	* SEM classes usually begin with "SEM:"

5 = Section Number (✔)
	* CAN BE EMPTY FOR PREVIOUS-CLASS LABS
	* Always an integer

6 = Course Title 
	* CAN BE EMPTY FOR LABS
	* Has multiple strings within it.

7 = Hour Credits
	* Always a double (Ala 1.0, 2.0, 3.0)

8 = Time {Beginning-End}
	* Always split between a '-' character into three (3) different sub-sections.
	* Some times include a 'N" to indicate it is a night class.
	* Should be removed of all spaces.

9 = Days {MTW TH F)
	* M = Monday
	* T = Tuesday
	* W = Wednesday
	* TH = Thursday
	* F = Friday
	{!} SA = Saturday (Not Currently Supported)
	{!} SU = Sunday (Not Currently Supported)

10 = Room
	* Always an string, due to the 0 in many of them and often letters included.

11 = Special Enrollment
	* OFTEN EMPTY, REGARDLESS OF LAB OR OTHERWISE

12 = Instructor
	* CAN BE EMPTY, REGARDLESS OF LAB OR OTHERWISE
	* Will be difficult to differentiate between professor and special enrollment requirements.
	* Alwayus consists of a longer string at the first half, and a single letter in the latter half.
